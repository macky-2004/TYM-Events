About TYM Events

We are not in the event business. We are in the family trust business.

Events are just the medium. Trust is the product. Every decision we make is filtered through one question: "Does this honor the family's trust?" We serve Hindu, Christian, and Muslim families across Tamil Nadu with equal dedication and cultural expertise.

Our Story

Born from Tamil Nadu's fragmented event landscape

After attending countless Tamil weddings, church ceremonies, family functions, and community events — we saw the same problem everywhere. Beautiful traditions, chaotic execution. Loving families, stressed coordinators. Rich culture, poor management.

We realized that no single platform existed that could handle the beautiful diversity of Tamil Nadu's events — from a traditional Hindu wedding with Vedic rituals to a church ceremony with full choir, a Muslim Nikah with Qazi coordination, or a family Pongal celebration. Each tradition demands unique expertise. Each family deserves premium execution.

We started as a traditional event management company serving Chennai families. We built our software internally because no tools understood Tamil Nadu's multi-community, multi-ritual reality. Then we realized the software was more scalable than the service. Today, we are Tamil Nadu's first technology-enabled event experience company — serving every community with equal respect and rigor.

Our Stand

What the Tamil Nadu event industry gets wrong — and how we fix it

Most event managers specialize in one community. Most over-promise and under-deliver. Most operate without systems. We refuse to play that game.

Single-Community Focus

Most event managers only understand one tradition. If a Hindu planner gets a Christian client, they miss the cultural nuances. We are equally expert across all Tamil Nadu communities.

Generic, One-Size-Fits-All

Families are treated as "just another event." Templates and checklists designed for one community are forced onto another. We create a custom operations playbook for every family and every tradition.

The Black Box Problem

Families give money and hope for the best. No transparency on vendor status, budget spend, or timeline progress. Our real-time dashboard makes everything visible, measurable, and controllable.

Hidden Costs & Lack of Contracts

"Wedding tax" and hidden vendor markups are common. Our industry is too informal, and families suffer. We are transparent about pricing from the first call — no surprises.

Unavailability When It Matters

Knowing you can message your coordinator at 10 PM before the wedding and get a response within 15 minutes — this is the highest-valued quality, yet rarely offered by traditional planners.

No Data, All Guesses

We use data. Before we plan, we benchmark against similar events from the same tradition — budget ranges, vendor costs, timeline benchmarks. We don't guess. We estimate with evidence from 300+ events.
