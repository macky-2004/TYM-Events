Service 01 Hindu Tradition

Hindu Wedding Management

Tamil Hindu weddings are among the most ritual-rich celebrations in the world. From the Muhurtham timing to the Saptapadi (seven steps), every ritual has deep meaning and precise requirements. We coordinate every aspect with the priest, family, and venue to ensure perfection.

Our coordinators are trained in Tamil Vedic traditions — Iyer, Iyengar, Mudaliar, Gounder, Naidu, and all Tamil Hindu communities. We understand the nuances of each sub-tradition.

  • Muhurtham time coordination with astrologer/priest
  • Full ritual management — Oonjal, Kanyadaanam, Thali tying, Saptapadi
  • Wedding hall/venue decoration with traditional floral themes (malai, pandhal)
  • Catering coordination — traditional Tamil vegetarian/naivedyam
  • Guest management for 100–5,000+ attendees
  • Live dashboard for family coordination across cities

Service 03 Muslim Tradition

Muslim Wedding & Family Events

Muslim weddings (Nikah) in Tamil Nadu are beautiful expressions of faith and family. We coordinate with the Qazi, manage the Mehndi (Henna) function, Nikah ceremony, Walima reception, and all associated events with cultural sensitivity and premium execution.

We also manage Ramzan Iftar parties, Bakrid family gatherings, Aqiqah celebrations, and other Muslim family events across Tamil Nadu.

  • Nikah coordination with Qazi and venue
  • Mehndi function planning and decoration
  • Walima (reception) management — catering, decor, stage
  • Iftar party and Ramzan event coordination
  • Bakrid family gathering management
  • Separate arrangements for men and women as needed
Service 04 Tamil Traditions

Traditional Ceremonies & Milestones

Tamil Nadu's traditional ceremonies are rich with meaning — from a baby's first rice (Anna Prasanam) to the 60th birthday (Shashtiabdapoorthi). Each ceremony has specific rituals, family roles, and traditional requirements. We preserve every custom with dignity and precision.

  • Seemantham / Valaikappu: Traditional baby shower with bangle ceremony, floral decor, family games
  • Upanayanam (Poonal): Sacred thread ceremony with full Vedic ritual coordination
  • Grihapravesam: Housewarming ceremony with pooja setup, kolam, traditional lamp
  • Anna Prasanam: First rice feeding ceremony for babies with traditional decor
  • Shashtiabdapoorthi / Sadhabhishekam: 60th and 80th birthday celebrations with pooja
  • Nischayatham: Engagement/betrothal function with ring ceremony and family gathering
